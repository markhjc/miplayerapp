Privacy Policy for MiPlayer

Last updated: October 5, 2024

Introduction

MiPlayer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our video player application (the "App") for iOS devices.

Information We Don't Collect

Our App is designed to function entirely locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Store or transmit your viewing history
  • Upload your media files
  • Use analytics tools
  • Connect to any servers
  • Share any data with third parties

Media Access

The App requires access to your device's media library solely to play the video files you choose. These files remain on your device and are never transmitted elsewhere.

Technical Data

The App operates completely offline. Any data generated during usage (such as playback settings or preferences) is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

App Store Privacy Details

In accordance with App Store guidelines, here is a summary of our data collection practices:

  • Data Not Linked to You: None
  • Data Used to Track You: None

Consent
